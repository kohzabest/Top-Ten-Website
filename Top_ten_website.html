Top Five Billy Joel Songs

1. Vienna 1977

This 1977 song of the "Stranger" album is one of Billy Joel's best songs. He expresses the feeling of calm and anxiety in one song. Though this was not a big hit single it still belongs as one of his best songs. My favorite line in this song is "Slow down, crazy child."
